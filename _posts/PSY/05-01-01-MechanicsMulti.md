#### **Player Configurations**
- Competitive 
	- 2-Player Games - Head-to-head competition. 
	- Multiplayer - Compete against others.
- Cooperative - Unite against game mechanics. (against the board)
	- parallel - own single player game along
	- step by step - 1 open for another
	- simultaneous - both action at same time
	- Team-Based - Work together toward goals.

- Symmetric/Asymmetric - Equal/unique powers. (King making)
- Campaign Play - Progress across sessions.
